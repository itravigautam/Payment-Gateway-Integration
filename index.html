A Wish is Hope, Donate Now to Help

We create life-changing wishes for children with critical illnesses and also provide food to the hungry children. We seek to bring every eligible child's wish to life because every child deserves a childhood. Our Foundation has become the world's leading children's wish-granting organization, serving children in nearly 30 countries worldwide. Together, generous donors, supporters, staff and more than 26,000 volunteers unite to deliver hope to wish kids and their families when they need it most. Please join us by making your donation to help us grant more life-changing wishes come true
